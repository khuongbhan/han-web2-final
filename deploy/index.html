papertown 2017:

the people

The People is a group of young people who share the same life motto: Start small, end big. We want to contribute even small things to our community. Our events promise to help you discover the limitless potentials of trivialities.

papertown

PAPERTOWN 2017 is a recycle-art exhibition consisting of many different activities (exhibition,workshops, mini-carnival, etc.). Through this event, we hope to spread the message to our community to cherish the smallest things around us.
